Track your donation's impact.

Social organisation sets goals

A social organisation consults with the people it helps to create a project that addresses their needs. It sets goals catering to these needs and presents the project for funding to donors, grant-making bodies or impact investors.

You donate to a project

A percentage of your donation is received upfront by the social organisation to deliver on the project. The rest of your donation is received every time the organisation completes a goal. If a goal is not fulfilled, you may choose to refund your donation.

Machine or human validates

Every time a social organisation completes a goal, it uploads evidence for a human validator to verify. Otherwise, it defines the data that triggers automated validation. Once a validation takes place, you receive a notification showing what your money achieved and the remaining sum of your donation gets received by the organisation.
